How to play

Unrevealed

Mine

An unrevealed tile might have a mine behind it, it might not. The idea is to clear all the tiles that don't have mines behind them.

But how are you supposed to avoid them? Here's how:

Cleared

Clue

If you avoid a mine, the number tells you how many of the 8 surrounding tiles are a black hole. If it's blank, none of the surrounding tiles is a mine.

If you think you know where a mine is, flag it!

Flagged

Active clue

Switch into flag mode, and tap the suspected tile. Once you've flagged enough tiles around a clue, it'll become active.

Tap an active clue to clear all the non-flagged tiles around it.

Shortcuts

If on PC, right-clicking a tile will Flag when in Clear mode, or Clear when in Flag mode.

If Clear mode is active, long-pressing a tile will Flag it.

If Flag mode is active, long-pressing a tile will Clear it.
